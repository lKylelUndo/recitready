# 📚 RecitReady

RecitReady is an AI-powered oral recitation and academic Q&A simulation platform designed to help students practice real classroom questioning, improve communication skills, and build confidence under pressure.

---

## 🧠 Problem

Students often struggle during:

- Oral recitations  
- Classroom reporting  
- Thesis / capstone defenses  
- On-the-spot questioning  

Even with knowledge of the topic, many lack:
- Confidence  
- Structured answering skills  
- Critical thinking under pressure  

---

## 💡 Solution

RecitReady simulates a real classroom environment using AI.

It helps students improve:
- Confidence in speaking  
- Critical thinking  
- Answer structuring  
- Academic communication skills  

---

## 🔄 System Flow

### 1. Authentication
Users can:
- Sign up / log in
- Maintain session history
- Track performance over time

---

### 2. Start Practice Session
Users input:
- Topic / lesson title  
- Optional discussion notes  

Example:
> Database Normalization

---

### 3. Difficulty Selection

- Easy → basic recall questions  
- Medium → analytical questions  
- Hard → deep critical thinking questions  

(Default: Easy)

---

### 4. Teacher Personality Mode

Users choose AI behavior style:

- 👨‍🏫 Friendly Teacher → supportive feedback  
- 📚 Strict Teacher → formal and critical  
- 🔥 Terror Teacher → high-pressure questioning  

---

### 5. AI Question Simulation

The AI generates questions based on:
- Topic
- Difficulty
- Teacher personality

---

### 6. Timer Pressure System ⏱

Each question includes a timer to simulate real recitation pressure.

Example:
- 30 seconds
- 1 minute
- 2 minutes

---

### 7. User Answer Input

Students respond via:
- Text input  
- (Future: voice input)

---

### 8. AI Evaluation & Feedback

AI evaluates answers based on:
- Correctness  
- Clarity  
- Completeness  
- Depth  

Provides:
- Improvement tips  
- Better answer suggestions  

---

### 9. Adaptive Follow-Up Questions

AI generates follow-up questions that increase in difficulty:

Example:
1. What is normalization?  
2. Why is it important?  
3. What happens if it is not applied?  

---

### 10. Performance Summary

At the end of each session:

- Overall score (0–100%)
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Improvement areas  

---

### 11. Practice History

Users can review:
- Past sessions  
- Questions & answers  
- Scores  
- AI feedback
